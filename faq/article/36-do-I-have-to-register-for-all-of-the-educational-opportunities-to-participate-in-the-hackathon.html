Do I have to register for all of the educational opportunities to participate in the hackathon?

No, the educational opportunities are designed to build your agency’s knowledge base of existing tools and strategies that can help your agency accomplish its mission. We encourage you to participate in the opportunities that will meet your agency’s needs. Although registration is not required to submit a proposal for the hackathon, we encourage all agencies considering a proposal to attend an informational session and register for coaching calls to help develop and refine proposals for the hackathon.
